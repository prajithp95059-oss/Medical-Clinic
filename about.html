Clinic Story

Started in one room. Built around one promise.

Stackly began in 2011 as a single consulting room above a pharmacy, opened by three physicians who were tired of seeing patients treated as case numbers.

Today, Stackly Healthcare anchors a network of 12 locations — but the founding promise hasn't moved an inch: every patient gets a doctor's full attention, not a rationed slice of it.

What started as a single room now runs on shared infrastructure — one patient record, one billing system, one standard of care — so a resident in Chennai and a resident in Coimbatore get the same exam, the same follow-up, and the same honest conversation about cost.

We measure ourselves less by how many patients we've seen and more by how many came back not because they had to, but because they wanted to.

Common Questions

Before your first visit.

Do I need a referral to see a specialist?+
No referral needed — you can book any specialist directly through the Doctors page or by calling our booking line.
Are all 12 clinics equipped for diagnostics?+
All locations run on-site labs; imaging (CT/MRI) is available at 8 of our 12 clinics, with same-day transfer for the rest.
Does Stackly accept insurance?+
Yes, we work with major insurance providers for cashless treatment — our front desk can verify your coverage before your visit.
Can I access my records from any clinic?+
Your chart is connected across all twelve locations, so any Stackly doctor can see your history the moment you check in.
