Questions

Frequently asked.

How quickly can I get a same-day appointment?

Most departments hold a handful of same-day slots that open at 7 AM. Call the department extension directly, or check live availability on the Doctors page.

Can I reach a doctor after hours?

The 24/7 emergency line above connects to on-call clinical staff. For non-urgent questions, message through your patient dashboard and expect a reply within one business day.

Do you offer telehealth consultations?

Select departments — including general medicine and follow-up cardiology visits — offer video consultations. Choose "Telehealth" as the visit type when booking.

How do I request my medical records?

Email hello@stacklyhealthcare.com with your registered phone number, or request a transfer directly from your patient dashboard.
