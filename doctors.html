Questions

Before you book.

Can I choose a specific doctor, or am I assigned one?

You choose. The booking form below lets you pick both department and physician by name — there's no random assignment.

What if my preferred doctor is fully booked?

The slot grid only shows genuinely open times for that doctor. If nothing works this week, our care coordinators can flag you for the next cancellation.

Do I need a referral to see a specialist?

No referral required for any specialty listed here — you can book directly with a cardiologist, orthopedist, or dermatologist.

Can I switch doctors after my first visit?

Yes, at any time. Your records carry over automatically to the new physician within the Stackly network.
